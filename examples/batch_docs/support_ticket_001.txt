Support Ticket #48502
Priority: High
Account: Nexus Analytics (Enterprise, 320 seats)
Submitted by: Omar Farouk, Head of Data Engineering
Date: July 10, 2026

Issue Description:
We are hitting the API rate limit every single day and it is causing significant
workflow disruption. Our data pipeline makes approximately 1,200 calls per hour
during business hours, which exceeds the current Enterprise cap of 1,000 calls/hour.

We are aware of the documented limit. The problem is that our use case has grown
substantially since we signed our contract 18 months ago. At the time, 1,000/hour
was more than sufficient. It no longer is.

Specific impact:
- Our nightly reconciliation job fails approximately 3-4 nights per week because
  the batch processing hits the limit partway through and cannot retry until the
  window resets.
- Our sales team's dashboard, which polls the API for real-time data, experiences
  blank screens or stale data for 10-15 minute windows during peak hours.
- We've had to implement hacky backoff logic that adds latency to user-facing features.

What we need:
1. A temporary limit increase to at least 2,000 calls/hour while we negotiate
   a revised contract tier.
2. Information on what an Enterprise Plus or custom rate limit tier would cost.
3. If a limit increase is not immediately possible, guidance on whether request
   batching is supported (combining multiple record fetches into a single API call).

We are up for renewal in 3 months. If this cannot be resolved before then, we
will need to seriously evaluate alternatives. I'd prefer not to do that — we
generally like the product — but this is blocking real business operations.
